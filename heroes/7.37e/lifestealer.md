### Open Wounds (Q)

**Type**: Unit Target

**Affects**: Enemy Basic, Enemy Hero
**Pierces Spell Immunity**: No
**Dispellable**: Yes

**Effects**:
- Lifesteal: 20% / 30% / 40% / 50%
- Max Slow: 35% / 40% / 45% / 50%
- Duration: 7 seconds
- Cooldown: 30 / 25 / 20 / 15
- Mana Cost: 75

**Cast Point**: 0.2s
**Cooldown**: 30 / 25 / 20 / 15
**Mana Cost**: 75

*Fresh wounds and the scent of blood often draw out the scavengers to finish the job.*
